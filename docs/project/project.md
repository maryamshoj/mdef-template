# Term1 

The first term focuses on exploring design through experimentation, collaboration, and reflection. Students begin by developing personal ideas and learning to design collectively, while understanding technological systems and extended intelligence. Alongside studio projects, they engage in reflective courses such as Narratives and Digital Gardening, which connect conceptual thinking with creative expression. The term also introduces interdisciplinary exploration through topics like Other Species and Extended Intelligence, emphasizing the relationship between nature, humans, and technology. Finally, students acquire hands-on skills in digital fabrication, learning how to transform their ideas into tangible prototypes and meaningful design outcomes.
